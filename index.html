Deities

Name
Mahadev
Creator, Destroyer, Transformer, Timeless, Supreme.

Mahadev, the Supreme Being, embodies creation, destruction,
and transformation. Timeless and formless, he dwells in deep meditation
yet fiercely protects dharma. Symbolizing inner peace and cosmic power,
he guides seekers toward liberation through devotion, discipline, and
detachment from ego.

Name
Parvati
Divine, Nurturing, Powerful, Graceful, Eternal

Parvati Mata, the divine consort of Mahadev,
embodies love,strength, and nurturing energy. As Shakti, she balances
the cosmos with compassion and power. A symbol of devotion
and motherhood, she guides souls through wisdom, grace,
and unwavering spiritual strength.

Name
Krishna
Playful, Wise, Divine, Compassionate, Eternal

Lord Krishna, the embodiment of divine love and wisdom,
enchants the world with his playful charm and profound teachings.
From guiding Arjuna in the Gita to spreading joy
through his leelas, he inspires devotion,
righteousness, and the path of truth.

Hanuman
Devotion, Love, Grace, Eternal, Soulful

Hanuman Ji, the mighty devotee of Lord Rama, embodies unwavering
devotion, immense strength, and fearless courage. Known for his

His presence inspires faith, discipline,
and fearless dedication to dharma.
